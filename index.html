How To Play

Explore by dragging your finger with VoiceOver off. The room stays fixed and your finger is you.

Containers open into explorable spaces. Trolleys are pushed, not carried. Items from shop aisles go into the trolley.

Children have safe placement rules: in the bath, in the cot, in the highchair, in the car seat. No sleeping on baths.

Sounds are location-aware. Babies do not broadcast across town. If you are away, babysitters or school can call you.

Driving has button controls and experimental tilt controls where your iPhone/browser allows it.
